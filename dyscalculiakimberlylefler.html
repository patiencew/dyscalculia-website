Kimberly Lefler

"As a teacher, I had a student dealing with dyscalculia."

As a teacher, I had a student dealing with dyscalculia. This website helped me understand what dyscalculia was and allowed me to get a better idea on how to help my students.

Once I understood what dyscalculia was and how it can affect a student's ability to understand math concepts, I was able to come up with creative ways to help my students learn. I started creating visual aids that explained complex math formulas in a simple, step-by-step way.

I also created interactive tools that students could use to practice and review material on their own time. My student with dyscalculia was able to benefit from these strategies, and I was able to see an improvement in their grades.
