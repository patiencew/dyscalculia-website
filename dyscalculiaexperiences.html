About Dyscalculator

Dyscalculator is a dyscalculia based learning site that strives to teach others about dyscalculia and spread awareness to this mathematical based learning disorder.

If you believe you may have dyscalculia, please reach out to our doctor or a therapist for possible assessments and/or accommodations.

Dyscalculia Facts

It's estimated that dyscalculia is at least 30 years behind in research compared to dyslexia!

Dyscalculia is also known as Developmental Dyscalculia!

In a class of 30 students, 1-2 may have dyscalculia.

While men and women both are diagnosed with dyscalculia in equal amounts, women are slightly more likely to be diagnosed with dyscalculia compared to men.
